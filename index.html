Who am I?

I am Amos Mwiti, a Kenyan born on April 15, 1999. My educational journey began at Nkumari Primary School, where I laid the foundation for my academic pursuits.
Primary Education: Attended Nkumari Primary School, completed KCPE in 2013 with 329 marks.
Secondary Education: Studied at Gikurune Boys High School and graduated with a C+ grade.
Further Education: Took a computer packages course at Kaguru Agricultural Center, sparking an interest in technology and business.
University: Joined Kirinyaga University in August 2018, earned a Bachelor of Business Information Technology degree with a Second Class Upper Division.
Future Goals: Focused on leveraging skills in technology and business to make a meaningful contribution to the field.

Life after School

After graduation, I struggled to secure a job despite my IT and business skills. I worked at a sewage company in Kirinyaga County, first digging trenches and later operating machinery. Following this, I ventured into banana cultivation. During this time, I took a self-learning course in data science and analytics, which sparked my passion for data analysis. Eventually, I transitioned into the business sector, specifically debt collection, where I applied my newfound skills to tackle financial challenges.
